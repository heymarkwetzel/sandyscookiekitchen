Frequently Asked Questions

If you don't see your question answered here, please do reach out!

How much are the cookies?

$12 per pound.

How many cookies are there in a pound?

20-25, depending on the types of cookies that go into your assortment.

Can I place an order for delivery?

Yes! We will deliver to you within 5 miles of Newburgh, NY (Walden, Montgomery, etc) for an additional fee of $5.00.

Do you sell in any coffee shops or bakeries?

Yes, of course! We proudly partner with The Bookmark Cafe at the Newburgh Free Library and Cafe Amelia's on Main Street in New Paltz, NY!

I own a cafe and would like to sell your goods in my shop. How do I do that?

Send Sandy an email or give her a call at 845-392-7787 to discuss! We love partnering with great cafes and would love to meet you!
